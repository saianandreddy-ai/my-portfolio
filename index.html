ABOUT

The story behind the engineer

I didn't start with AI. I grew into it.

My journey began writing backend code in Hyderabad, debugging APIs late into the night, learning how systems talk to each other. Back then, intelligence in software meant a well optimized SQL query. I had no idea that a decade later I would be building systems that think.

Over 10 years, I moved through every layer of the stack. From legacy Python migrations to real time Kafka pipelines, from fraud detection models to enterprise LLM copilots serving thousands of users. Each role taught me something the previous one could not. Healthcare taught me precision. Banking taught me trust. Retail taught me scale. Insurance taught me consequence.

Today I work at the intersection of language models and real world decisions. I build RAG pipelines that help underwriters make faster calls. I design GenAI systems that process claims documents in seconds instead of hours. I architect retrieval systems that do not just find information but understand context.

What drives me is not the technology itself. It is the moment a workflow that used to take a team three days gets done in three minutes. That is the version of AI I am interested in. Not the demo, but the deployment.
